COMPANY OVERVIEW

STEMCOM TECHNOLOGIES is a Tanzanian based company specializing in ICT (Information & Communication Technologies) Solutions and ICT (Information & Communication Technologies) Consultancy. The company was established in August 2010, and located at Stanford House along New Bagamoyo Road/Hekima Street, Plot No: 2058 Block D, Dar es Salaam.

STEMCOM TECHNOLOGIES aims at providing highly demanded solutions and business services for the enterprise sector, covering a wide range of activities. Our solutions can be tapped and exploited for the benefit of home users, small business, medium to large busy IT environments. We are what we make Innovative, Reliable and Efficient.

STEMCOM TECHNOLOGIES earned a reputation for high performance and reliability that is based on providing our customers with ICT products and services that are constructed from quality components and provide reliability to a wide range of industries.

Our vision

The Company aims at addressing the technological challenges by providing innovative services and solutions that meet higher quality standards in Tanzania. With a definite orientation towards market needs, and being aware of the research issues to be addressed, the main ambition is to offer reliable solutions to its partners and satisfy the scientific interests of its members.

Our mission

Is to serve the power needs of our customers with high quality products and services. We pride ourselves on the care that goes into the construction of each unit. All of our products are designed in-house; this ensures that we have control over quality at all stages of production. The result is that our customers are receiving a product they rely on – ALWAYS.

We see obstacles as challenges to find new and exciting ways to fulfill our customers' power needs. Our team of engineers is continually researching and developing new technology to allow our product line to evolve with the changing needs of the industries.

Our philosophy

Our approach is simple – we refuse to compromise when it comes to quality. We set our standards high and we aim to surpass them. We do this by investing in the continuous improvement of our products and services by building a team that is knowledgeable and dedicated to ensuring that we exceed the power needs of our customers .

Our goals

At STEMCOM TECHNOLOGIES, we develop standards and set goals that help contribute to our success and your satisfaction.

These are the goals that we have set for ourselves:

  • Our goal is to be available for our customers when they need us.
  • To build lasting relationships they can rely on.
  • To connect with new business and continue to grow into the new century.
  • To continually enhance our services with a knowledgeable and friendly support staff.
  • To create a trouble-free experience for our customers.
  • be a leader in the power industry.

"Our goal is success and success for ICT means listening to our customers, understanding their needs and then working to find solutions that satisfy these needs."

Our values

Excellence: We strive to be excellent in the methods we use to conduct our business with continual process improvement

Professionalism: We offer our customers a number of standard service level agreements geared to meeting different technical support requirements

Integrity: We honour fair practices, ethics, honesty and offer our full commitment

Innovation: We will continually create and improve our products, services, and delivery techniques for all our customers

Teamwork: we promote effective teamwork in the workplace and strongly support it in each individual's goals

Customer Satisfaction: It is our personal responsibility to satisfy and strive to exceed the expectations of our customers

OUR TEAM

All company members are Computer Engineers, graduates from various colleges and universities. Our individual technical and scientific skills, in addition to sole proprietorship experience, resulted in a highly-qualified research team working in close collaboration.
